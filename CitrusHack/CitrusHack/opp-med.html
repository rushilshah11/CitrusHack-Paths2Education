Stanford Medicine Cardiothoracic Surgical Skills and Education Center Summer Internship

The Cardiothoracic Surgical Skills and Education Center Stanford Summer Internship is designed to educate high school students considering careers in science, medicine, and public health.

Students learn about:

• Cardiovascular anatomy and physiology

• Medical and surgical techniques that will be utilized in pre-medical and medical school









Coriell Institute for Medical Research Summer Internships

Coriell Institute invites a limited number of motivated students to participate in a Summer Experience program to gain insight into the workings of a renowned research institute.

Program details:

• Four-week program that runs during the month of July

• A stipend of $1000 is awarded to each student upon completion of the program
