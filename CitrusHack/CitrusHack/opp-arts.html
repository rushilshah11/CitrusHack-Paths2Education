The Met Summer High School Internship

The Metropolitan Museum of Art offers paid summer intenrship opportunities for rising juniors and seniors in New York, New Jersey, or Connecticut

Students can:

• Explore the world of professional art and interact with career professionals in the visual arts

• Apply strong knowledge of art history





The Smithsonian

The Smithsonian offers a wide variety of internship opportunities.

Areas of interest include:

• Fine art

• Art history

• Museum administration
